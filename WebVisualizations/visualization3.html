Cloudiness

According to the data, the correlation between latitude and cloudiness is very weak to none. Latitude does not appear to have any appreciable affect on how cloudy a city is, particularly in the northern hemosphere. While weak, southern hemosphere latitudes appears to be slightly more correlated to cloudiness, where cities closer to the equator are slightly more cloudy.
